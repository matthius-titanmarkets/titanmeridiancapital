# 05 · Compliance Calendar

> The office's recurring obligations in one place. Exact filings depend on
> entity types, elections, and jurisdictions — **have counsel and the tax
> advisor confirm this list annually**, then keep it current here. Items
> below are the common shape for a US single-family office with an LLC
> trading division; yours may differ.

---

## 1. Monthly

| When | Item | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| 5th business day | Reconciliation & statement publishing (Runbook §3) | Ops |
| Mid-month | Payroll/contractor filings if applicable | Ops |
| Month-end | Sales/use & state obligations if applicable | Ops |

## 2. Quarterly

| When | Item | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Q close + 15d | Estimated federal/state tax payments (entities & individuals) | Tax advisor |
| Q close + 30d | Family letter & distribution documentation | CIO / Ops |
| Each quarter | Broker statement archive completeness check | Ops |

## 3. Annually

| When | Item | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| January | IPS & Charter re-ratification; access review; vendor audit | IC |
| Jan–Apr | Entity tax returns & K-1s; individual returns | Tax advisor |
| State deadline | LLC/entity annual reports & franchise fees (each state of formation/registration) | Counsel/Ops |
| Anniversary | Registered agent renewals; DBA renewals | Ops |
| Anniversary | Domain, hosting, and certificate renewals (see 07) | Ops |
| Mid-year | Insurance review (property, liability, cyber, D&O if applicable) | Ops |
| Any year with changes | Beneficial-ownership / corporate-transparency filings where required | Counsel |

## 4. Event-driven (file when triggered)

- Entity formation/dissolution; change of registered agent or address.
- New brokerage/bank account → update the account register (07 §5) and the
  reconciliation map the same week.
- Distribution outside the standing programme → committee minute + capital
  account entry before funds move.
- Trust events (funding, trustee change, beneficiary change) → counsel
  drafts, archive the executed documents.
- Material software change to execution (bot strategy/risk parameters) →
  signed config change record (Runbook §2).

## 5. Standing posture notes

- The office manages **family capital only** and does not hold itself out to
  the public. Preserve that posture in all published materials — it is
  load-bearing for the office's regulatory footprint (confirm annually with
  counsel, especially if anything changes: outside investors, performance
  marketing, or paid advice would change the analysis entirely).
- Books and records: keep trade logs, statements, minutes, and letters for
  at least [7] years; never edit archived records — append corrections.
