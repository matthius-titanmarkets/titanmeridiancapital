# Titan Meridian Capital — Family Office Build Pack

The operating manual of the office, written down. Each document is a working
template: adopt it, date it, and have the investment committee sign it.

> **Standing disclaimer.** These documents are internal operating templates,
> not legal, tax, or investment advice. Entity formation, trust drafting,
> regulatory status, and tax elections must be reviewed with qualified counsel
> and tax advisors in your jurisdictions before adoption.

| # | Document | Purpose |
|---|----------|---------|
| 01 | [Master Blueprint](01_master_blueprint.md) | What the office is, org map, service pillars, build sequence |
| 02 | [Investment Policy Statement](02_investment_policy_statement.md) | Objectives, mandates, risk budgets, rebalancing, prohibited actions |
| 03 | [Governance Charter](03_governance_charter.md) | Investment committee, roles, decision rights, meeting cadence |
| 04 | [Operations Runbook](04_operations_runbook.md) | Daily→annual operating calendar, reconciliation, reporting standards |
| 05 | [Compliance Calendar](05_compliance_calendar.md) | Recurring filings, reviews, and renewal checkpoints |
| 06 | [Onboarding Checklist](06_onboarding_checklist.md) | Bringing a family member, entity, or counterparty into the estate |
| 07 | [Technology Estate](07_technology_estate.md) | Every system the office runs, where it lives, who owns it |
| 08 | [Security & Continuity](08_security_continuity.md) | Access control, key management, incident response, succession |

## How to use this pack

1. Read **01** end-to-end once — it explains how the pieces interlock.
2. Adopt **02** and **03** formally: fill in names, sign, date. These two
   documents are the constitution of the office.
3. Run the calendars in **04** and **05** as living checklists.
4. Revisit everything at the annual governance meeting (see 03 §6).
