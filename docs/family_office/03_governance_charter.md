# 03 · Family Governance Charter

**Adopted:** ____________  **Review:** annually (January)

> Template for internal adoption; align entity-specific authority (managers,
> trustees, signers) with counsel before signing.

---

## 1. Bodies & roles

| Body / Role | Held by | Authority |
|---|---|---|
| **Investment Committee (IC)** | [Principal], [Member 2], [Member 3] | Owns IPS, risk doctrine, mandate weights; hires/fires vendors |
| **Chief Investment Officer** | [Principal] | Day-to-day allocation within IPS bands; desk oversight |
| **Head of Trading** | [Name] | Runs the desk within the risk doctrine; owns the bot's configuration |
| **Operations & Reporting** | [Name] | Reconciliation, statements, portal publishing, calendars |
| **Family Council** | All adult members | Receives reporting; ratifies charter changes affecting beneficiaries |

One person may hold multiple roles in a lean office, but the **reconciliation
duty (Ops) and the trading duty (Desk) must not be the same person's sole
review** — the monthly reconciliation is checked by a second set of eyes,
even if that second set is a committee member reviewing exports.

## 2. Decision rights

| Decision | Authority | Record |
|---|---|---|
| Trade within doctrine | Desk / bot (automatic) | Trade log (automatic) |
| Rebalance within bands | CIO | Journal entry |
| Band breach action | IC majority | Minute + journal |
| Amend IPS / risk doctrine | IC unanimous, 7 days' notice | Signed amendment |
| New asset class / vehicle | IC unanimous + counsel review | Minute + counsel memo |
| Distributions & contributions | IC majority per standing programme | Capital account entry |
| Emergency halt | Any IC member, immediately | Journal; ratify within 48h |

## 3. Meeting cadence

- **Weekly desk review** (30 min): drawdown state, limits, event risk ahead.
- **Monthly ops review** (60 min): reconciliation sign-off, allocation vs.
  bands, statement approval before portal publishing.
- **Quarterly family meeting**: letter walkthrough, mandate scoring,
  distribution confirmation.
- **Annual governance meeting** (January): IPS re-ratification, charter
  review, vendor and access review, continuity drill scheduling.

Minutes: one page per meeting, decisions and owners only, stored in the
estate archive (07 §5).

## 4. Reporting standard

- Monthly capital statement per account: beginning balance, flows, P&L,
  ending balance — reconciled to custodians before publishing.
- Quarterly letter: performance vs. objectives, allocation, amendments,
  outlook. Written even in bad quarters — *especially* in bad quarters.
- The portal mirrors, never replaces, the archived statements of record.

## 5. Conflicts & discipline

- Personal trading in instruments the desk holds requires disclosure to the
  IC and same-day logging.
- Vendor relationships with any family connection are disclosed and minuted.
- Breaches of the risk doctrine — human or software — trigger a written
  post-mortem within [5] business days regardless of P&L outcome.

## 6. Succession & amendments

- Each role names a successor in the continuity plan (08 §6).
- Charter amendments: IC unanimous + Family Council majority, 14 days'
  notice, recorded with rationale.
