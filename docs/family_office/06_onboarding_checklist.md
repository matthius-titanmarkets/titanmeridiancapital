# 06 · Onboarding Checklist

Three flows: a family member joining the reporting circle, a new entity or
account entering the estate, and a new counterparty/vendor.

---

## A. Family member (reporting & portal access)

- [ ] Family Council introduction; walk through the Charter one-on-one.
- [ ] Explain the reporting rhythm: what arrives monthly/quarterly and what
      each figure means (teach the capital statement line by line once).
- [ ] Issue portal access **personally** (no emailed credentials; see 08 §2).
- [ ] Record contact, preferred channel, and distribution instructions in
      the family register.
- [ ] Standing invitation to the quarterly meeting; calendar invite sent.
- [ ] Log completion in the annual governance minute.

## B. New entity or account

- [ ] Committee resolution authorizing the entity/account (Charter §2).
- [ ] Counsel confirms formation documents, ownership map updated (01 §2).
- [ ] Banking/brokerage opened with **dual control** where supported.
- [ ] Added to: account register (07 §5), reconciliation map (Runbook §3),
      backup scope (08 §4), and the compliance calendar (05).
- [ ] Signers and API access documented; least-privilege applied.
- [ ] First reconciliation completed within one month of first activity.

## C. Counterparty / vendor

- [ ] Due diligence note: who they are, what they access, exit cost.
- [ ] Contract reviewed; auto-renewal dates into the compliance calendar.
- [ ] Access granted least-privilege; credentials in the vault (08 §2).
- [ ] Conflict disclosure if any family connection (Charter §5).
- [ ] Annual vendor audit inclusion (Runbook §5).

## D. Offboarding (mirror image — run it the same day status changes)

- [ ] Revoke portal/systems access; rotate any shared secrets they touched.
- [ ] Close or re-title accounts; final reconciliation.
- [ ] Archive correspondence and final statements.
- [ ] Minute the completion.
